Key Learning: Real estate marketing isn't about lead volume — it's about matching the buyer segment to the right creative and trust-building mechanism. Three completely different audiences, three different approaches, one combined ₹10.6L spend producing ₹90.91+ Crores in attributed sales across luxury, mid-market, and premium residential.

Chennai Fertility Centre — Free IUI Funnel
Direct IVF ads were tested first and discontinued fast: ₹2,222 CPL vs ₹272 CPL once the Free IUI Consultation replaced them — an 8X lead-volume jump at 87% lower cost. IUI naturally converts to IVF after 3–4 cycles, and because trust was already built with CFC's doctors, 214 of 487 IUI patients progressed to IVF at the same clinic — at zero additional ad spend.
Pearl Singapore Fertility Centre — "100 Days" Funnel
Generic "Best IVF Centre" ads returned a 0.6% CTR and ₹580 CPL. The "Pregnant in 100 Days" campaign — a phase-by-phase, doctor-led treatment plan — lifted CTR to 2.8% and dropped CPL to ₹276. 602 leads, 240 qualified, 5 IVF conversions on ₹2L spend, with PSFC becoming the first Chennai fertility brand to run a structured 100-day plan.
WhatsApp Nurturing — The Highest-Leverage Fix: At CFC, leads without follow-up booked consultations at a 31% rate. A structured WhatsApp sequence — confirmation, education, success stories, progressive IVF education — lifted that to 70%, a 126% improvement. At PSFC, consultation show-up rate rose from 58% to 86% once WhatsApp confirmation and pre-visit education were added. Same lever, same result, two different clinics.
Key Learning: In high-cost, high-anxiety categories like fertility treatment, the offer at the top of the funnel matters more than the ad spend behind it. Removing the financial barrier (CFC) and removing the timeline uncertainty (PSFC) both outperformed advertising the procedure itself — proof that the right entry offer beats the right targeting every time.
